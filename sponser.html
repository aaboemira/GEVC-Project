Software Sponsorship

The GEVC student teams must conduct rigorous engineering analyses and simulation to innovate, optimize their designs, meet the safety regulations and develop their race strategies. We are excited that our software partners are offering technical support and access to their exceptional software tools to the university teams. Software licenses, in addition to access to a wealth of online tutorials, videos and webinars, are offered to GEVC registered teams in the form of “team sponsorship”. We encourage you to take advantage of these resources to advance your knowledge and capabilities in your discipline of study, and of course to enhance your vehicle design and race performance.

The Altair CAE suite with its leading edge simulation technology in the fields of light weight design, aerodynamics, safety, manufacturing, electromagnetics and in combination with our passion for motorsports, teaching and technical support will help you design the difference. To learn more visit Altair University - GEVC and to request sponsorship from Altair visit Competitions and Sponsorship Information.

MathWorks, maker of MATLAB and Simulink, prepares and supports the next generation of scientists and engineers with software, training, and mentoring to tackle the same technical issues as professional engineers. GEVC teams receive industry-standard tools, with a flexible design environment where they can apply classroom theory to competition problems. Find out more about how technical computing and Model-Based Design can help you compete by visiting Mathworks Academia – GEVC.
